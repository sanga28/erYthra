About Erythra

Erythra is a revolutionary platform that aims to transform the way blood donation is perceived and executed in emergency and routine healthcare. It uses technology not just for connection, but for empathy, impact, and life-saving coordination.

Through AI-driven matchmaking, live location tracking, blockchain transparency, and inclusive design, we bring together donors, recipients, hospitals, and blood banks into a seamless ecosystem. Our app ensures that no one is left behind, regardless of language, region, or resources.
